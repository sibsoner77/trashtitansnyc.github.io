NYC Apartment Prep Tips

Make your junk removal smooth and efficient with these NYC-specific preparation tips

🏢

Reserve Your Elevator

Contact your building management 24-48 hours ahead to reserve elevator access and avoid delays.

📋

Notify Your Building

Inform your super or doorman about the scheduled pickup to ensure smooth building access.

🚛

Check Parking Rules

We handle parking permits when needed, but knowing your street's restrictions helps us plan better.

📦

Group Your Items

Collect all items in one area near your entrance to speed up the removal process.

💎

Check for Valuables

Double-check drawers, pockets, and compartments for any personal items before we arrive.

🚪

Clear the Path

Ensure hallways and doorways are clear for safe and efficient item removal.
